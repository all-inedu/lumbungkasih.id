Who Are We?

"Lumbung Kasih" is an Indonesian non-profit organization founded on kindness, compassion, and selfless service. Our name, translated as "Barn of Love", symbolizes our commitment to gathering resources and extending support to those who need it the most, just like a barn provides for the community in times of need. We are a beacon of hope for the less fortunate, a platform of endless possibilities transcending geographical, financial, and social barriers.

Our work is diverse and extends to various aspects of aid. Still, our goal remains focused: to alleviate suffering, empower the marginalized, and inspire a ripple effect of kindness and generosity in society. From arranging transport for children to receive their COVID-19 vaccines to funding university education for deserving students, and providing monthly rice donations to children with special needs, Lumbung Kasih is on a constant mission to find and seize opportunities to help.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision is to create an empathetic and supportive world where no one is left out of the growth story, where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has a fair chance at a wholesome life, brimming with opportunities and hope.

Our Mission embodies three core objectives :

1
Empowerment

To foster self-reliance and confidence among those we serve, by providing necessary resources and support, thus enabling them to take control of their lives and futures.

2
Education

To break the cycle of poverty and disadvantage through the power of education, ensuring that promising students have access to quality learning and skill development opportunities, irrespective of their financial background.

3
Health and Well-being

To promote health and well-being by ensuring that even the most vulnerable members of society have access to essential healthcare services, including vaccinations and treatments for ongoing conditions.

At Lumbung Kasih, we believe in the power of collective compassion and action. We strive to create a society that extends a hand to help, a heart to empathize, and a spirit to uplift those around us. We are Lumbung Kasih, and we are here to help - anytime, anywhere, anyhow.

Funding the education of unfortunate kids is essential for multiple reasons. Firstly, education is a basic human right, and every child, regardless of their circumstances, should have access to it. Secondly, education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. It equips children with the knowledge and skills needed for a productive future. Lastly, when we invest in a child's education, we're not just changing their life, but also contributing to societal growth, economic progress, and global stability. Every child we help today becomes a better-prepared adult tomorrow, influencing positive change for generations to come.
